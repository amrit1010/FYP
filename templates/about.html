Welcome to Ramsa bajar

Ramsa Bajar is an online marketplace where people can buy, sell, rent, and auction different products and services. It ensures security and trust by verifying users through email and KYC (Know Your Customer) checks. Vendors can only add products after completing their KYC verification, making transactions safer. Users can easily browse, purchase items, and update their profiles, while vendors can manage their products with options to add, edit, or delete listings. With a simple and user-friendly design, Ramsa Bajar aims to make online shopping and trading easier and more reliable in Nepal.
